3000-2500 B.C. Aryans from the Central Asia came in contact with the HP areas; Crossed Purushani (Ravi) and reached Arijikiya (Beas). The Aryan King Divodas defeated the Kiratâ€™s King Sambhar which lasted 40 years. This war found mention in the Rigveda.

500 A.D. Kullu state ruled by Raja Brahmpal .

1000 A.D. Tomar Rajput Jethal founded Nurpur state with its capital at Pratistan (Pathankot).
