# Raja Kailash Pal   
* Dynasty-or-Surname:: `{_obsidian_pattern_tag_last}` Pal, ruled till 1450, After him, there seem to have no Raja in Kullu for about 50 years, after Kailash Pal `{_obsidian_pattern_tag_district/kullu}` was controlled by Suket, Ranas, and Thakuras.
